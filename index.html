Leh To Srinagar

Ladakh (meaning "land of high mountain passes") is a mountainous region in North India. Leh, the main city is situated at 3500m above sea level nestled in the great Himalayan, Karakoram, Zanskar and Ladakh ranges. Ladakh is known for remote mountain beauty, studded with monasteries and stupas.

Parashar Lake & Tunga Mata Trek

ituated at an altitude of 8956 feet above mean sea level, Parashar Lake is an hallowed lake in the Mandi district of Himachal Pradesh. It is believed that the lake has no bottom or no scientist has been able to measure the depth of the lake.

Dayara Bugyal

Dayara Bugyal is grass land situated in the Uttarkashi district of Uttarakhand in India, starting at an altitude of 2600 mtrs and goes upto 3500 mtrs (11482 ft) above sea level. 'Bugyal' literally means a high altitude meadow in the local language of the area.

Malana To Rasol Trip

Malana is an ancient Indian village in the State of Himachal Pradesh. This solitary village is situated in Parwati Valley to the north-east of Kullu district isolated from the rest of the world. Malana is known for being one of the oldest democracies in the world guided by their devta (deity) Jamlu rishi.

than(sone ki thali) is an isolated village in Hamta valley, Himachal Pradesh. This small Buddhist village is situated at 2700m above sea level, this tiny hamlet is far away from the hustle and bustle of Manali and yet easily accessible.
